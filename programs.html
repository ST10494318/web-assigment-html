Our Programs in Action

Discover how we're educating the next generation and restoring our local environment.

School Workshops

Environmental Education Workshops

Our interactive workshops bring environmental science to life for learners. We cover topics like recycling, water conservation, and local biodiversity, empowering youth to become eco-conscious citizens.

Key Features/Benefits:

  • Designed for grades 5-12
  • Curriculum-aligned content
  • Hands-on activities

Community Clean-Up Projects

We organize regular clean-up events in parks, rivers, and beaches around Cape Town. These events bring the community together for a hands-on day of making a visible difference.

Key Features/Benefits:

  • Open to all volunteers
  • All equipment provided
  • Corporate team-building opportunities
